# About

I am Song Ziqi, a PhD student in Electronic Science and Technology at Nankai University.

My research focuses on micro/nano devices, with interests in device design, fabrication, and physical mechanisms of novel electronic devices.

## Education

- PhD in Electronic Science and Technology, Nankai University
- Previous education: The undergraduate major is Microelectronics Science and Engineering, from Anhui University.

## Biography

My current work is centered on micro/nano-scale electronic devices and related research topics in electronics and device physics.
