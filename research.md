# Research

## Research Interests

### Micro/Nano Devices
Research on the design, fabrication, and characterization of micro/nano-scale electronic devices.

### Device Physics
Study of transport mechanisms, interface effects, and structure-property relationships in functional devices.

### Novel Electronic Devices
Exploration of emerging device architectures and their potential applications.
